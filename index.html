Welcome to the Satcom Sation Project

This is a joint project between Derek Kozel (@dkozel) and Jerzy Dziewierz (@jerzydziewierz) to design and build a satellite communications ground station based on the SatNOGS project (@satnogs).

Antennas

The first antenna is a UHF Helical antenna tuned for 437 MHz. It is based off of the SatNOGS design, but the 3D printed components have been redesigned and resized.
